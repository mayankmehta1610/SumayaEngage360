# Jurisdiction workflow completion matrix

The global-mobility module is configuration and workflow support, not legal advice. Rules can change and may vary by state, province, emirate, occupation, workforce size, establishment type and EU member state. Administrators must review the linked authority guidance and obtain local advice where needed.

| Jurisdiction | Employer intake | Candidate and payroll profile | Work authorization | Verification and monitoring |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| India (primary/default) | Legal entity, PAN/TAN/GSTIN, EPFO/ESIC, state, establishment and worksite registrations | Identity/contact, PAN/UAN/ESIC, tax declaration, bank/nomination, state of employment | Citizen, OCI, employment/project/intern and other authorized routes | Identity/visa/FRRO, statutory enrollment, state compliance, renewal and governed exit |
| United States | EIN, state payroll/unemployment, workers compensation, E-Verify/worksite and wage controls | Identity/contact, SSN reference and Form I-9 document path | Citizen/PR/EAD and principal temporary-worker routes | Form I-9 timing, E-Verify, authorized-until, reverification and retention |
| United Kingdom | Company/PAYE/pension, sponsor licence and work location | Identity/contact, NI reference, share code and student term dates | Citizen/settled, sponsored and non-sponsored work routes | Online/document/ECS check, outcome, repeat-check date and retention |
| Canada | CRA/provincial payroll, workers compensation, LMIA/portal and NOC/TEER | Identity/contact, SIN reference/expiry, province and medical needs | Citizen/PR, LMIA, IMP and open permits | Permit employer/location/occupation conditions, expiry and employer compliance retention |
| Australia | ABN/PAYG, super, workers compensation, Fair Work award and sponsorship | Identity/contact, TFN reference, consent and skills evidence | Citizen/PR and major sponsored/open routes | VEVO date/reference, visa conditions and expiry monitoring |

## Controls available in every jurisdiction

- Common employer, legal entity, worksite, payroll, social-insurance, workplace-insurance, privacy owner and retention fields.
- Common candidate identity, contact, address, emergency contact, banking, consent, evidence and accommodation fields.
- Draft, ready-for-review and verified employer profiles with server-side mandatory-field checks.
- Draft, ready-for-review and complete candidate profiles with server-side mandatory-field checks.
- Controlled work-authorization transitions, verification evidence, restrictions, checklists, sponsorship and expiry monitoring.
- Search, column filters, sorting, pagination, Excel export and PDF/print export on web tables.
- Country setup, employer/candidate profiles, authorization cases, search and pagination in the mobile app.
- Report RPT-026 for employer/candidate completeness, status and country distribution, open cases, 90-day expiries and expired open cases; report output is exportable to Excel and PDF.
